Lab Partnership

Lupin Diagnostics Accredited Laboratory

Spectrum Family Clinic operates as an official collection and diagnostic partner of Lupin Diagnostics. This guarantees that every blood or urine sample processed is tested in state-of-the-art automated diagnostic labs, maintaining strict international accuracy protocols.

Hassle-Free Home Sample Collection

Skip the morning clinic rush and fasting travel discomfort. Our certified clinical phlebotomist will visit your residence at your preferred time slot to collect blood samples safely and hygienically.

  • Highly trained phlebotomists with painless vacuum collection tubes
  • Temperature-controlled sample transport to prevent degradation
  • Digital reports sent directly to your WhatsApp and Email
