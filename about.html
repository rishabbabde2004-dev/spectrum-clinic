About Our Clinic

Our Journey

Dedicated to Family Wellness

Spectrum Family Clinic & Diagnostic Centre was established in Gudimalkapur, Hyderabad, with a clear focus on making premium, comprehensive outpatient medical consultations and diagnostic services accessible all under one roof.

We understand that visiting separate centers for consulting a doctor, getting blood tests, and receiving physiotherapy is exhausting. Therefore, we structured Spectrum to integrate these three essential verticals, backed by top consultants from premier local healthcare institutions like Olive Hospital and Osmania Hospital.

Today, we serve thousands of local families, delivering clinical accuracy through our partnership with Lupin Diagnostics, and offering convenient home sample collections and online consultations.
